Company Policies

1. Privacy Policy:
   We value your privacy. Customer data is stored securely and is never shared without consent.

2. Refund Policy:
   Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned product is received.

3. Shipping Policy:
   Orders are shipped within 2 business days. Tracking information is provided via email.

4. Code of Conduct:
   All employees must treat customers with respect and professionalism at all times.

5. Data Retention Policy:
   Customer data is retained for up to 5 years as per compliance requirements.
